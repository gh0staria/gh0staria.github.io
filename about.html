About

Hi! I'm Liz Wigglesworth. I'm a Computer Science student working towards my B.S at the University of Texas at Dallas.

I write code at BroadcastSIMS, an educational software startup that gives broadcast teachers the tools they need to prepare their students for the live production industry.

My GitHub also has side projects I've worked on over the years. They range from small example repos to semester-long projects. The ones I'm proudest of are in my portfolio.

Some of my interests in technology are...

  • cybersecurity in general
  • digital forensics
  • cryptography
  • distributed tech & the p2p web
  • computer literacy & technical education

But of course, I'm not all about technology. Some other things I do are...

  • Learning languages! If I wasn't interested in computer science, I probably would've been a linguist. The way that languages work and how they're connected to each other fascinates me. I'm currently focusing on maintaining/improving my Spanish (B1) and learning Dutch (A1).
  • Gardening! I've accumulated quite a few houseplants over the years and I'm slowly expanding into growing plants I can use in the kitchen. I also volunteer at the UT Dallas Community Garden.
